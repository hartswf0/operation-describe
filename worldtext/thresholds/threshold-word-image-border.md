# Threshold: The Word/Image Border

> **Species**: threshold  
> **Scale**: fragment  
> **Primary World**: [[world-classical-ekphrasis]]  
> **Last Revision**: 2026-04-13  

---

The fundamental crossing between verbal and visual representation. The border that classical ekphrasis exists to inhabit, cross, and problematize. Mitchell's entire framework (hope/fear/indifference) is about the experience of standing at this border.
