# Threshold: Actual / Notional

> **Species**: threshold  
> **Scale**: fragment  
> **Primary World**: [[world-classical-ekphrasis]]  
> **Last Revision**: 2026-04-13  

---

The crossing between describing a real artwork (actual ekphrasis) and describing an imaginary one (notional ekphrasis). Homer's Shield is notional — it exists only in the poem. Keats's urn may be actual or notional. This distinction is fundamental to classical ekphrastic theory.
