# Threshold: Sequential / Connectionist

> **Species**: threshold  
> **Scale**: fragment  
> **Primary World**: [[world-operative-ekphrasis]]

Bajohr's (2024) structural partition of the digital realm. In the sequential regime (classical algorithms), text and image remain distinct. In the connectionist regime (neural networks), they collapse. This threshold marks where operative ekphrasis becomes technically possible.
