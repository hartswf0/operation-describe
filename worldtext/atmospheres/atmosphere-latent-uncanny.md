# Atmosphere: Latent Uncanny

> **Species**: atmosphere  
> **Scale**: fragment  
> **Primary World**: [[world-latent-space]]  
> **Last Revision**: 2026-04-13  

---

The eerie feeling when AI-generated images almost-but-not-quite match reality — six-fingered hands, spatially impossible architectures, faces that blur at the edges. Generated by the gap between the model's statistical coherence and physical/biological reality.

## Generating Structures

- The distance between training data distribution and real-world distribution
- The lossy compression of VAE encoding ([[place-latent-space]])
- The guidance-diversity tradeoff: high guidance produces sharp but uncanny results

## Evidence Base

- Multiple sources on AI uncanny valley, implosion in text-to-image (source indices 500, 690)
