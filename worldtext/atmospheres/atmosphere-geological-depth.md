# Atmosphere: geological depth

> **Species**: atmosphere  
> **Scale**: fragment  
> **Primary Worlds**: [[world-classical-ekphrasis]], [[world-thick-description]]  
> **Last Revision**: 2026-04-28  

---

The atmosphere of deep time made present. When a synthesis traces the genealogy of ekphrasis from Homer through Lessing through Mitchell to Bajohr, the reader feels compressed layers of intellectual sediment — each layer visible but compressed. The atmosphere is not of speed but of accumulation.

This atmosphere dominates the Heritage Cluster (classical-ekphrasis + shield-of-achilles + oral-formulaic-generation). These worlds carry thousands of years of inherited form. The geological depth is what makes them authoritative — and what makes them resistant to the new (the AI regime sits on top of this sediment but cannot dissolve it).
