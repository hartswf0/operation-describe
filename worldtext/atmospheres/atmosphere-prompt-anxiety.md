# Atmosphere: Prompt Anxiety

> **Species**: atmosphere  
> **Scale**: fragment  
> **Primary World**: [[world-prompt-craft]]  
> **Last Revision**: 2026-04-13  

---

The uncertainty about whether the next prompt will yield the intended output. Generated by the opacity of the model's interpretation process: the prompter cannot see how their words map to the latent space.

## Generating Structures

- The intent/output gap ([[threshold-human-machine]])
- The non-deterministic nature of sampling (temperature, seed)
- The opacity of transformer attention mechanisms
- Prompt sensitivity findings showing small word changes produce drastically different outputs

## Evidence Base

- Multiple sources on prompt sensitivity (source indices 110, 661)
- Lindley & Whitham 2025 on uncertainty as material condition
