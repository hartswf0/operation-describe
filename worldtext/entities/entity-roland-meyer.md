## Identity

Roland Meyer. Media theorist. Author of "Platform Realism" (2025) and "Images from Images" (*Photography and Culture*, 2025). The critical counterpart to the operative ekphrasis project — Meyer provides the socio-technical diagnosis that constrains the project's claims.

## Key Contributions

1. **[[concept-platform-realism]]**: AI image synthesis produces a second-order aesthetic of generic content — images optimized for legibility, plausibility, and pastness. The latent space reflects platform capitalism, not creative vision.

2. **Navigation vs. production**: Meyer's key inversion of the operative ekphrasis claim. Prompts "find" images rather than "produce" them — they navigate a [[concept-latent-space]] that was shaped by platform capitalism long before the prompter typed a word. See [[distinction-navigation-production]].

3. **[[concept-synthetic-camera]]**: The three-way distinction between material camera (photography, indexical), virtual camera (3D rendering, optical simulation), and synthetic camera (AI, no fixed location, no optical laws, only statistical patterns).

4. **Remediation extended**: Meyer extends Bolter & Grusin's remediation framework, citing Jay Bolter's own 2023 IMAGE article. AI remediates not media *forms* but aesthetic *vibes* — nameable atmospheric patterns extracted from the corpus.

5. **Abundance as waste**: Meyer provides the critical counterpart to [[entity-watson-hartsoe]]'s affirmative [[concept-abundance]]: mass AI overproduction is also waste, visual pollution, and a self-consuming loop.

## The Challenge to the Cosmos

Meyer's argument is the most direct challenge to the cosmos's central claims. If:
- The word navigates rather than produces → "operative" is a misnomer
- The latent space is politically pre-structured → the prompter's agency is constrained
- AI remediates vibes rather than media → the tradition is absorbed, not extended

The cosmos must absorb this challenge without flattening it. Platform realism is not a contradiction of operative ekphrasis but its **political-economic shadow** — the gravitational field that bends every trajectory in the latent space.
