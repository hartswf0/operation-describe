Renate Brosch is a literary scholar who has argued for updating ekphrasis theory for the **digital age**.

## Key Contributions

- **"Ekphrasis in the Digital Age"** (2018) — Argues for a meta-representational and rhetorical conceptualization of ekphrasis that accounts for participatory, hybrid digital practices. Traditional ekphrastic description has been "replaced by modes of rewriting the artwork."
- **"Ekphrasis in Recent Popular Novels"** (2018) — Examines how contemporary fiction reauratizes art through ekphrastic affect and transformation.
