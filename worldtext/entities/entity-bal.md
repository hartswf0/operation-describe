Mieke Bal (b. 1946) is a cultural theorist and artist who advocated for **travelling concepts** — analytical tools that cross disciplinary borders and transform in transit.

## Key Contributions

- **Travelling Concepts in the Humanities** (2002) — Treats ekphrasis as a concept that travels across disciplines, functioning as cultural translation and affective engagement.
- **Quoting Caravaggio** (1999) — Uses ekphrastic strategies to discuss how contemporary art reinterprets historical artworks through "preposterous history."
