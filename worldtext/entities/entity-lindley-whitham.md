Joseph Lindley and Roger Whitham are design researchers who proposed the reframing of **prompt engineering** as **prompt craft**.

## Key Contributions

- **"From Prompt Engineering to Prompt Craft"** (TEI 2025) — Through three practice-based design research projects with Stable Diffusion, they argued for understanding prompting as a **craft practice** involving tangible, embodied interaction with the materiality of generative AI. Key concepts: materiality of diffusion, navigation of latent space as craft, strong concepts as intermediate knowledge.
