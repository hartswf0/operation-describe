Liliane Louvel is a literary theorist who introduced the concept of the **iconotext** and the **pictorial third**.

## Key Contributions

- **Poetics of the Iconotext** (2011) — The "iconotext" is the hybrid zone where word and image merge. Important for European approaches to ekphrasis.
- **"Types of Ekphrasis"** (2018) — Proposed a functional typology: narrativizing, maieutic, hermeneutic, subversive, elegiac, and affective ekphrasis. Introduced the "pictorial third" — a mental image generated by the oscillation between text and visual artwork.
