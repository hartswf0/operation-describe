David Ha and Jürgen Schmidhuber are the authors of **"World Models"** (2018), a foundational paper demonstrating that RL agents can learn compressed spatial-temporal world models and train inside their own hallucinated dreams.

## Key Contributions

- **"World Models"** (2018) — Built a generative neural network (VAE + MDN-RNN) that learns a compressed representation of an RL environment. The agent can then be trained **entirely inside its own hallucinated dream**, with the learned policy transferring back to the real environment.
- This has cosmological significance: the agent's dream-world is an **ekphrastic object** — a generated representation of reality that the agent inhabits. It is both description and environment.
