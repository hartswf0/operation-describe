## Poles

| Seamless | Exposed |
|----------|---------|
| Photorealism, hidden mediation | Multiplicity foregrounded, process visible |
| Ekphrastic hope: the word commands a truly real image | Ekphrastic fear as aesthetic: word/image dissolution is the subject |
| Immediacy (Bolter & Grusin) | Hypermediacy (Bolter & Grusin) |
| The viewer should NOT know the image is AI-generated | The viewer should SEE the act of generation |
| Default of [[concept-platform-realism]] | Resistance to platform realism |
| Krieger's natural sign illusion, now technically sustained | Louvel's pictorial third, now technically produced |

## The Question

Will the system **hide** or **expose** the act of world-compilation? This is the decisive aesthetic question of operative ekphrasis.
