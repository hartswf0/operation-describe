# Distinction: representation / performance

> **Species**: symbolic_distinction  
> **Scale**: fragment  
> **Primary World**: [[world-classical-ekphrasis]]  
> **Last Revision**: 2026-04-13  

---

**Poles**: representation / performance

Does ekphrasis represent its visual object mimetically, or perform/enact it? Ancient rhetoric (Webb) emphasized performance (enargeia); modern theory (Heffernan) emphasized representation. Digital ekphrasis inherits both.
