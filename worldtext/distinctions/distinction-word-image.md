**Poles**: word / image

The foundational binary. Language is temporal, arbitrary, sequential; images are spatial, motivated, simultaneous (per Lessing 1766). Active across all ekphrastic worlds. Technically collapsed in operative ekphrasis (Bajohr 2024) but experientially persistent.
