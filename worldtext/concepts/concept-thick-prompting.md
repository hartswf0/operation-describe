## Definition

Thick prompting is the application of Geertz's thick description to the act of writing prompts. A thin prompt specifies format and subject ("a woman in a kitchen"). A thick prompt layers context, code, tradition, audience, evaluative frame, and reclassification conditions.

The six layers of a thick prompt:

| Layer | Name | What It Does |
|-------|------|-------------|
| 1 | **Visible act** | What the image shows (subject, composition, action) |
| 2 | **Mood/atmosphere** | The feeling the image carries (tone, lighting, emotional register) |
| 3 | **Code** | The style tradition or genre convention invoked (art-historical, cinematic, photographic) |
| 4 | **Audience** | Who evaluates this and by what standard (gallery, client, community, self) |
| 5 | **Context** | The situation this image enters (portfolio, argument, archive, conversation) |
| 6 | **Reclassification** | What this image does to the category it belongs to (confirms, challenges, extends, destroys the genre) |

Most prompts operate at Layer 1 only. A thick prompt operates across all six. The thickness is what separates prompt-craft from prompt-typing.

## Why It Matters

A thin prompt produces [[concept-platform-realism]] — the default aesthetic of the training distribution. A thick prompt wrestles the model away from its center of mass. The thickness is the [[concept-viscosity]] the operator introduces deliberately.
