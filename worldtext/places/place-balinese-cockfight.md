# Place: The Balinese Cockfight

> **Species**: place  
> **Scale**: place  
> **Primary World**: [[world-thick-description]]  
> **Last Revision**: 2026-04-13  

---

Geertz's paradigmatic scene of thick description (1973). A Balinese cockfight arena where gambling, status-competition, masculine display, and social hierarchy are woven into a cultural "text" that the ethnographer must read for its semiotic density, not merely observe for its behavioral patterns.

The most-cited ethnographic scene in social science. It persists as an ur-memory throughout the galaxy of thick description worlds.
