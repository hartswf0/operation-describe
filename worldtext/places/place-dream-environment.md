# Place: The Dream Environment

> **Species**: place  
> **Scale**: place  
> **Primary World**: [[world-world-models]]  
> **Last Revision**: 2026-04-13  

---

The hallucinated training world inside a neural world model. Ha & Schmidhuber (2018) showed AI agents can be trained entirely within this generated reality, then transfer learned policies to the real environment. An ekphrastic object that exists solely to be inhabited.

## Evidence Base

- Ha & Schmidhuber 2018 (source index 43)
