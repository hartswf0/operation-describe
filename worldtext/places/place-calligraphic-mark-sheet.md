# Place: The Calligraphic Mark Sheet

> **Species**: place  
> **Scale**: place  
> **Primary World**: [[world-intermediality]]  
> **Last Revision**: 2026-04-13  

---

Thomas Ingmire's sheets of calligraphic marks that trigger cascading intermedial transformations: marks → poems → artist's book (via Japanese paper-folding) → musical composition. Kennedy (2018) argues this is ekphrasis at its limits — and precisely where intermediality begins.
