# World-Type Taxonomy

> **Species**: taxonomy  
> **Scale**: cosmos  
> **Last Revision**: 2026-04-13  

---

## Discovery Criteria

A world-type is named when:
- At least 3 sources or world objects share a deep structural pattern
- The pattern includes recurring places, thresholds, rituals, infrastructures, and symbolic distinctions
- The pattern is not adequately captured by existing types

---

## Active World-Types

### 1. paragone-representation-world

**Parent type**: none (root)  
**Signature ontology**: Worlds organized around the structural competition between two representational media (classically word vs. image).  
**Signature places**: gallery wall, museum, poem margin, stage  
**Signature entities**: poet, painter, artwork, verbal description, visual image  
**Signature rituals**: ekphrastic description, art criticism, paragone debate  
**Signature atmospheres**: agonistic tension, aesthetic longing, representational anxiety  
**Signature infrastructures**: canonical artworks, literary tradition, art historical apparatus  
**Signature thresholds**: word/image border, actual/notional crossing  
**Signature symbolic distinctions**: word/image, stasis/movement, verbal/visual  
**Signature governance**: canon formation, art historical hierarchy, aesthetic judgment  
**Signature flows**: attention, prestige, interpretive authority  
**Signature conflicts**: paragone, representation vs. performance  
**Worlds instantiating it**: world-classical-ekphrasis, world-shield-of-achilles  
**Influence weight**: 90

### 2. latent-space-navigation-world

**Signature rituals**: diffusion sampling, gradient descent, interpolation, ablation  
**Signature atmospheres**: latent uncanny, computational sublime, noise-to-clarity emergence  
**Signature infrastructures**: GPU clusters, model weights, training datasets, checkpoint files  
**Signature thresholds**: noise/signal crossing, dimension reduction boundary, training/inference border  
**Signature symbolic distinctions**: noise/signal, latent/manifest, continuous/discrete  
**Signature governance**: benchmark tables, loss functions, evaluation metrics  
**Signature flows**: gradients, attention scores, training data, compute  
**Signature conflicts**: fidelity vs. diversity, control vs. emergence  
**Worlds instantiating it**: world-latent-space, world-world-models  
**Influence weight**: 85

### 5. operative-ekphrasis-world

**Parent type**: paragone-representation-world + latent-space-navigation-world (hybrid)

**Signature places**: digital canvas, multimodal model interior, text-image encoder  
**Signature entities**: multimodal AI, text-image embedding, DALL·E, Stable Diffusion, operative ekphrast  
**Signature rituals**: prompt-to-image generation, text-image alignment, cross-modal translation  
**Signature atmospheres**: semiotic vertigo, generative awe, uncanny emergence  
**Signature infrastructures**: transformer architectures, CLIP, multimodal training pipelines  
**Signature thresholds**: description/generation collapse point, text/image encoding boundary, sequential/connectionist border  
**Signature symbolic distinctions**: description/generation, text/image (collapsed), human/machine authorship  
**Signature governance**: content moderation, model alignment, intellectual property regimes  
**Signature flows**: prompts, embeddings, generated images, feedback loops  
**Signature conflicts**: description vs. generation, authorship attribution, semiotic residue  
**Worlds instantiating it**: world-operative-ekphrasis  
**Influence weight**: 95

### 11. oral-formulaic-world

**Parent type**: paragone-representation-world + latent-space-navigation-world (hybrid)  
**Signature ontology**: Worlds where knowledge is composed, stored, and transmitted through formulaic pattern-completion — from Homeric oral epic to LLM autoregressive generation. The structural operation is the same: select the next unit from a learned distribution of inherited patterns.  
**Signature places**: performance ground, formulaic inventory, composition threshold  
**Signature entities**: oral-formulaic poet, Homeric bard, Parry, Lord, Avdo Medjedovic, LLM, training corpus  
**Signature rituals**: oral performance, formulaic selection, autoregressive generation, real-time composition  
**Signature atmospheres**: bardic electricity — the hum of a pattern-completing system at compositional capacity  
**Signature infrastructures**: oral tradition, metrical grammar, epithet systems, statistical weight matrices, tokenizers  
**Signature thresholds**: oral/literate crossing, singer/machine boundary, formula/token border  
**Signature symbolic distinctions**: memorized/composed, formula/token, tradition/individual  
**Signature governance**: metrical constraint, audience feedback, training loss  
**Signature flows**: formulaic phrases, attention weights, metrical conformity, narrative momentum  
**Signature conflicts**: tradition vs. individual talent, convention vs. novelty  
**Worlds instantiating it**: world-oral-formulaic-generation, world-shield-of-achilles  
**Influence weight**: 80

**Discovery note**: Bridges the oldest galaxy (ekphrastic) to the newest (generative AI) through Parry-Lord theory. The Shield of Achilles was not written but generated by an oral-formulaic composition system. Operative ekphrasis is the return of the oral-formulaic condition at computational scale. 8 sources confirm the pattern.

### 12. embodied-description-world

**Parent type**: thick-description-world + prompt-craft-world (hybrid)  
**Signature ontology**: Worlds where description is somatic rather than verbal — gesture, touch, body prompting, haptic feedback, kinesthetic response. The medium of the ekphrastic encounter is the body, not language.  
**Signature places**: performance body, somatic interface, dance floor, installation space  
**Signature entities**: gesturing body, body prompt, motion capture system, haptic controller  
**Signature rituals**: body prompting, gestural ekphrasis, somatic inscription  
**Signature atmospheres**: somatic electricity — the proprioceptive hum of a body in creative motion, sweating, trembling, adapting  
**Signature infrastructures**: cameras, motion sensors, haptic controllers, VR rigs, installation spaces  
**Signature thresholds**: verbal/somatic crossing, screen/body transition, typing/gesturing border  
**Signature symbolic distinctions**: typing/gesturing, somatic/semiotic, mind/body, precision/expression  
**Signature governance**: phenomenological attentiveness, embodied ethics, consent of the moving body  
**Signature flows**: gesture, movement, proprioceptive feedback, visual output, bodily fatigue  
**Signature conflicts**: mind vs. body, control vs. surrender, verbal precision vs. gestural expressiveness  
**Worlds instantiating it**: world-embodied-description  
**Influence weight**: 70

**Discovery note**: Gives the cosmos a body. Dissolves the text-centrism of operative ekphrasis by introducing the body as a third term (body → latent space → image). 6 sources confirm the pattern. Hawhee (2011) demonstrates ekphrasis was always somatic; Oppenlaender (2024) makes it computationally operative.
