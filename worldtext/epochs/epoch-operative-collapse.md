## Description

The epoch that begins with the public release of text-to-image models (DALL·E 2, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion in 2022) and the subsequent theoretical recognition (Bajohr 2024) that multimodal AI has **collapsed the distinction between describing and generating images**. This is the era in which:

- The classical paragone implodes rather than resolving
- Prompting becomes a new form of ekphrastic practice
- Latent spaces become the new sites of image-making
- "Thick description" begins to travel into AI evaluation
- The academic ekphrasis world scrambles to update its definitions
