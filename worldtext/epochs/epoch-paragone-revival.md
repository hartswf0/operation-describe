# Epoch: The Paragone Revival (1766 – 1994)

> **Species**: epoch  
> **Scale**: system  
> **Period**: 1766 – 1994  
> **Locale**: European literary-critical tradition  
> **Last Revision**: 2026-04-13  

---

From Lessing's *Laocoön* (1766) through the Romantic ekphrastic paradigm (Keats, Shelley) to Mitchell's *Picture Theory* (1994). The paragone is codified, debated, and reframed as cultural anxiety. Heffernan (1993) and Krieger (1992) anchor the field; Mitchell (1994) reconfigures it.
