# Epoch: Ancient Rhetoric (800 BCE – 500 CE)

> **Species**: epoch  
> **Scale**: world  
> **Period**: 800 BCE – 500 CE  
> **Locale**: Ancient Mediterranean  
> **Last Revision**: 2026-04-13  

---

The foundational epoch. Ekphrasis originates as a rhetorical exercise (*progymnasmata*) — the art of making absent things present through vivid description (*enargeia*). Homer's Shield of Achilles (Iliad XVIII) is the ur-text. Philostratus's *Imagines* extends the tradition to prose descriptions of paintings.
