# Epoch: The Intermedial Turn (1997 – 2018)

> **Species**: epoch  
> **Scale**: system  
> **Period**: 1997 – 2018  
> **Locale**: International comparative literature, media studies  
> **Last Revision**: 2026-04-13  

---

Clüver (1997) pushes ekphrasis beyond verbal/visual to all intersemiotic transposition. Rajewsky (2002) systematizes intermediality theory. By 2018, *Poetics Today* Vol. 39 No. 2 publishes a special issue that reconfigures the field — Brosch, Jansson, Mathieson, Kennedy, Bundschuh, and others expand ekphrasis into digital, performative, and participatory forms.
