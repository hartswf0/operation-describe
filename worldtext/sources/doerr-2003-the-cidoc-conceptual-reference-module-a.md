## Abstract / Summary

This article presents the methodology that has been successfully used over the past seven years by an interdisciplinary team to create the International Committee for Documentation of the International Council of Museums (CIDOC) conceptual reference model (crm), a high‐level ontology to enable information integration for cultural heritage data and their correlation with library and archive information. The CIDOC crm is now in the process to become an International Organization for Standardization (ISO) standard. This article justifies in detail the methodology and design by functional requirements and gives examples of its contents.
