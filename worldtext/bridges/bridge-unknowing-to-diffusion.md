## The Process Parallel

**Wright's Unknowing**: The poet empties their mind → approaches the image with "evenly dispersed attention" → allows forms to emerge gradually through sustained looking → the poem resolves from the encounter.

**Diffusion Denoising**: The model starts with pure noise → applies learned denoising at each step → allows forms to emerge gradually through iterative refinement → the image resolves from the process.

```
UNKNOWING (Wright)                    DIFFUSION (AI)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Empty mind                           Pure Gaussian noise (t=T)
    ↓                                    ↓
Receptive attention                  First denoising steps
    ↓                                    ↓
Vague shapes, moods emerge           Coarse structure appears
    ↓                                    ↓
Specific details crystallize         Fine details resolve
    ↓                                    ↓
The poem                            The image (t=0)
```

Both processes are **iterative**, **gradual**, and **emergent**. Both resist the temptation to jump to a final form too quickly. Both produce their best results when the agent (poet/model) is genuinely open to what emerges rather than pre-committed to an outcome.

## The Temperature Connection

| Temperature (AI) | Equivalent (Wright) |
|:----------------:|:-------------------:|
| t = 0 (deterministic) | Fully "knowing" — the poet projects known tropes onto the image |
| t = 0.3 (focused) | Guided looking — some structure, some openness |
| t = 0.7 (balanced) | Bion's "capacity to forget" — structured unknowing |
| t = 1.0 (random) | Pure unknowing — no preconception at all |

Wright implicitly argues for t ≈ 0.7: not total ignorance (which would produce nothing) but **structured emptiness** — enough architectural knowledge to recognize forms, but not enough to predict them.

## Keats's Negative Capability

This bridge extends beyond Wright to Keats's **negative capability**: "when a man is capable of being in uncertainties, mysteries, doubts, without any irritable reaching after fact and reason."

Negative capability IS the humanistic name for high-temperature generation:
- No irritable reaching after fact = no greedy decoding
- Being in uncertainties = sampling from the full distribution
- Without reaching after reason = allowing the associative process to unfold without constraint

## The Noise Is Not Nothing

The crucial insight: in diffusion models, the "noise" at t=T is not random nothingness. It is **structured by the model architecture** — it occupies a specific region of latent space conditioned by the text prompt. Similarly, Wright's "unknowing" is not emptiness. It is **structured by the poet's training, sensibility, and bodily presence** — an emptiness that has a specific shape.

The noise = the poet's shaped receptivity. Not absence but potential.
