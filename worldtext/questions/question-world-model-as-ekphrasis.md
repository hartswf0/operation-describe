# Question: Are Neural World Models Ekphrastic Objects?

> **Species**: question  
> **Scale**: world  
> **Frontier**: Is the model's internal simulation a described world or a generated one?  
> **Filed**: 2026-04-13  

---

Ha & Schmidhuber's (2018) world models train RL agents inside hallucinated environments. The hallucinated world is both a representation (it models the real environment) and a generation (it creates novel experiences). This makes the world model a candidate for the first truly operative ekphrastic object — a representation that is also an environment.
