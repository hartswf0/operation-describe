# Ritual: Thick Inscription

> **Species**: ritual  
> **Scale**: fragment  
> **Primary World**: [[world-thick-description]]  
> **Last Revision**: 2026-04-13

Layering contextual meaning onto observed behavior — the core epistemic act of thick description. Geertz's ritual of reading the "wink" as socially meaningful (rather than recording the "twitch" as behavioral fact). Stabilizes the world of thick description by maintaining the commitment to semiotic density over behavioral parsimony.
