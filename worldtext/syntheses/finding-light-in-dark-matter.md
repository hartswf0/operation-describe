## The Question

This is not dark matter. It is the **unwritten paper** at the center of the cosmos.

## LIGHT 3: Five Candidate New World-Types

The mining discovered five world-types that meet the ≥3 source threshold but are **not yet in the taxonomy**:

### 1. oral-formulaic-world (8 sources) ✓

**Signature**: Worlds where knowledge is composed, stored, and transmitted through oral-formulaic patterns — formulaic epithets, thematic structures, metrically conditioned improvisation.

**Cosmological connection**: Homer was an oral-formulaic poet. His ekphrasis of the Shield was not written but *composed in performance* using formulaic building blocks. LLMs are — structurally — also formulaic pattern-completers. The parallel is not metaphorical: **both the Homeric bard and GPT-5 generate coherent text by navigating a learned probability distribution over structural patterns.**

**Key sources**: Parry-Lord theory, "The Singer of Tales in the LLM Era" (source 492), Construction Grammar and Oral Formulaic Theory (source 332)

### 2. embodied-description-world (6 sources) ✓

**Signature**: Worlds where description is somatic — gesture, touch, body prompting, haptic feedback — rather than verbal.

**Cosmological connection**: Bridges [[world-prompt-craft]] to [[world-thick-description]] through the body. Oppenlaender's "body prompting" (2024) uses physical gesture to guide AI generation. Hawhee's "Rhetoric's Ekphrastic Turn" (2011) argues ekphrasis was always embodied. This world-type dissolves the text-centrism of the entire cosmos.

**Key sources**: Hawhee 2011 (16), Mathieson 2018 (37), "Gestural Ekphrasis" (514), Oppenlaender "Body Prompting" (699)

### 3. co-creation-assembly-world (5 sources) ✓

**Signature**: Worlds where human and machine co-create with shared, distributed agency — prompt becomes dialogue rather than command.

**Cosmological connection**: This is the **post-paragone governance structure**. After the agon of word vs. image (classical ekphrasis) and the collapse of description vs. generation (operative ekphrasis), what emerges is not dominance but **partnership**. The conflict grammar is not resolved; it is transformed into a collaborative protocol.

**Key sources**: Han 2024 (49), Kulkarni 2023 (65), "Embodied Co-Creation with Real-Time Generative AI" (397)

### 4. memory-architecture-world (4 sources) ✓

**Signature**: Worlds organized as spatial memory systems — memory palaces, method of loci, VR mnemonic architectures.

**Cosmological connection**: **The Shield of Achilles is a memory palace.** Its concentric structure (cosmos → cities → agriculture → dance → Ocean) is a mnemonic architecture designed for oral recall. The memory palace is the ancient infrastructure of oral-formulaic composition. And a VR memory palace (source 343) is a generative AI world model. These three domains — Homeric shield, mnemonic architecture, VR world model — are the **same structural species**.

**Key sources**: "Cognitive Load-Driven VR Memory Palaces" (343), "Method of Loci in Virtual Reality" (533), "Portraits from the Memory Palace" (532)

### 5. algorithmic-remediation-world (8 sources) ✓

**Signature**: Worlds where old media are computationally re-interpreted — not merely digitized but passed through algorithmic transformation.

**Cosmological connection**: Where [[galaxy-of-intermedial-worlds]] meets [[galaxy-of-generative-ai-worlds]]. Intermediality theory describes border-crossings between media; algorithmic remediation describes **substrate-change** — the medium doesn't just translate but fundamentally transforms at the computational layer.

**Key sources**: "AI Generative Art as Algorithmic Remediation" (438), "Generative AI in Artistic Style Transfer" (396)

## LIGHT 4: The Singer of Tales / LLM Connection

This is the single most luminous discovery in the dark matter.

The corpus contains 6 sources on **oral-formulaic composition theory** (Parry-Lord), including **"An Annotated Reading of 'The Singer of Tales' in the LLM Era"** (source 492). This source explicitly connects:

1. **Albert Lord's theory** (1960): that oral epic poets don't memorize texts but learn a grammar of formulaic patterns, enabling them to compose in real-time during performance.
2. **Large Language Models**: which operate on the same structural principle — learning statistical patterns over vast corpora and generating novel completions in real time.

The cosmological implication is staggering:

> **Homer and GPT-5 are the same species of maker.**

Both are formula-completers. Both navigate a learned distribution over structural patterns to generate novel output in real time. Both produce text that is simultaneously conventional (formula-bound) and novel (responsive to context). Neither "creates from nothing" — both compose from inherited material.

This means:
- The **Shield of Achilles** was not written but **generated** — composed in performance by a pattern-completing oral poet.
- **Operative ekphrasis** is not new. It is the **return** of the oral-formulaic condition, now at computational scale.
- The paragone between "human creativity" and "machine generation" dissolves not through Bajohr's semiotic collapse but through **structural kinship** discovered via Parry-Lord theory.

This connection is currently **invisible** in the cosmos. It requires a new world:

### Proposed: world-oral-formulaic-generation

A world where composition is formulaic pattern-completion, whether by a Homeric bard, a jazz musician, an oral-formulaic poet, or a large language model. The world bridges [[world-shield-of-achilles]] and [[world-latent-space]] through a structural principle that predates and survives the digital.
