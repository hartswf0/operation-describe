## Ontology

Electronic literature, kinetic text, screen-based ekphrasis, and digital poetry that uses remediation to create new forms of word-image interaction. This world inherits from both classical ekphrasis and intermediality but adds the unique affordances of digital media: movement, interactivity, multimodality, and code-as-substrate.
