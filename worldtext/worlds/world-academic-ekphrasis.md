## Ontology

This world is not *about* ekphrasis — it is the **scholarly apparatus** that produces, circulates, governs, and contests ekphrastic theory. It is the academic world that houses the other ekphrastic worlds. Its places are seminar rooms, journal offices, conference panels, and citation networks. Its primary labor is the maintenance of the field itself.

## Governance

- **Definitional authority**: who defines ekphrasis?
  - Heffernan 1993: "verbal representation of visual representation" — narrow, paragonal
  - Clüver 1997: intersemiotic transposition — broad, intermedial
  - Webb 1999: ancient rhetorical enargeia — historical, performative
  - Brosch 2018: meta-representational, participatory — contemporary, digital
  - Bajohr 2024: operative — collapses the entire frame

## Conflict Grammars

- [[conflict-canon-vs-expansion]] — The field has been locked in a 30-year debate about whether to narrow or broaden the definition of ekphrasis.
- Disciplinary boundary disputes — Comparative literature vs. art history vs. rhetoric vs. digital humanities.
