## Ontology

This is the world where the human-machine interface is the **prompt** — a linguistic artifact composed, refined, and iterated to invoke specific generative outputs. Lindley & Whitham (2025) propose a pivotal reframing: from **prompt engineering** (technical optimization) to **prompt craft** (material practice). The prompt is not a command but a *making* — a shaping of language that engages with the materiality and uncertainty of generative AI.
