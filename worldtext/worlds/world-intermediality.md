## Ontology

The world of intermediality is organized around what happens **at the borders between media, sign systems, and representational modes**. Where classical ekphrasis focuses on the word/image border specifically, intermediality theory addresses all intersemiotic crossings: text ↔ image, text ↔ music, performance ↔ inscription, analog ↔ digital. The key insight: crossing between media is never neutral. It transforms both the source and the target.
