# Conflict: Representation vs. Performance

> **Species**: conflict_grammar  
> **Scale**: fragment  
> **Primary World**: [[world-classical-ekphrasis]]  
> **Last Revision**: 2026-04-13  

---

Whether ekphrasis represents its visual object (mimesis) or performs it (enactment). Webb (1999, 2016) recovered the ancient performative tradition; Heffernan (1993) anchored the representational model. Hawhee (2011) pushed further toward embodied, sensory performance. This conflict generates movement within classical ekphrasis theory.
