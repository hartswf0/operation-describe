# Conflict: Canon vs. Expansion

> **Species**: conflict_grammar  
> **Scale**: fragment  
> **Primary World**: [[world-academic-ekphrasis]]  
> **Last Revision**: 2026-04-13  

---

The tension between preserving classical ekphrastic canons (Heffernan's narrow definition, the traditional paragone) and admitting new forms (intersemiotic ekphrasis, digital ekphrasis, operative ekphrasis). This conflict has structured the academic ekphrasis world for 30 years: every new theory pushes the boundary; every defense of the canon pushes back.
