# Conflict: The Paragone

> **Species**: conflict_grammar  
> **Scale**: fragment  
> **Primary World**: [[world-classical-ekphrasis]]  
> **Last Revision**: 2026-04-13  

---

The structural competition between word and image for representational supremacy. The oldest conflict grammar in the cosmos. Originates in the sister-arts tradition and Lessing's *Laocoön* (1766), codified by the ut pictura poesis debate. The paragone does not resolve — it generates the entire galaxy of ekphrastic worlds through its irresolvability.
