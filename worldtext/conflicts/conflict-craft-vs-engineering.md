# Conflict: Craft vs. Engineering

> **Species**: conflict_grammar  
> **Scale**: fragment  
> **Primary World**: [[world-prompt-craft]]  
> **Last Revision**: 2026-04-13  

---

Whether prompt-writing is creative craft (embodied, material, uncertain) or technical optimization (efficient, measurable, systematizable). Lindley & Whitham (2025) argue for craft; the prompt optimization literature (AutoPrompt, LatentPrompt) assumes engineering. This conflict animates the world of prompt craft.
