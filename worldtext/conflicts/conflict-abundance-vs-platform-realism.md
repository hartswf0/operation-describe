## The Tension

**Abundance** (Watson-Hartsoe) and **platform realism** (Meyer) are two readings of the same phenomenon: mass AI image overproduction.

| Abundance (affirmative) | Platform Realism (critical) |
|------------------------|---------------------------|
| AI generates infinite variations = creative richness | AI generates infinite variations = visual pollution |
| The ekphrast samples from a distribution = freedom | The distribution was pre-structured by platform capitalism = constraint |
| Multiplicity enables exploration | Multiplicity produces the mean ([[entity-hito-steyerl]]) |
| The exposed path ([[concept-seamless-vs-exposed]]) celebrates this | The seamless path hides it; the critical framework exposes it |
| [[concept-reactive-ekphrasis]] = generative chains | Reactive ekphrasis → convergence to 12 generic motifs ("visual elevator music") |
| The human navigates possibility space | The space navigates the human toward its defaults |

## What Generates the Tension

The tension is irreducible because both accounts are empirically accurate at different scales:

- **At the level of a single prompt session**: abundance is real. The prompter explores, discovers, is surprised. Creative agency is experienced.
- **At the level of the population**: platform realism is real. Billions of generated images converge toward stock-photo aesthetics, nostalgic vibes, and legible genericity. The training distribution dominates.

The question is not which account is right but **at what scale** you observe. Abundance is the micro-phenomenology; platform realism is the macro-sociology. The cosmos must hold both without collapsing either.

## The Overview's Question

> How should the paper navigate the tension between abundance (affirmative) and platform realism (critical) as framings of AI overproduction?

The cosmos's proposed answer: the paper does not need to choose between them. The tension is itself the argument. The **thick prompter** operates within both: they experience abundance (micro) while navigating a pre-structured space (macro). Thick prompting is the practice of exercising agency *within* constraint — which is also the definition of craft, of rhetoric, and of ethnographic embedding.
