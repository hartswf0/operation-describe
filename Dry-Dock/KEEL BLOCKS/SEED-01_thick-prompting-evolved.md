## EVOLVED TEXT (327 words)

Media scholars who study generative AI still treat the prompt as a command. This is an error that costs the field its most productive analogy.

Geertz (1973) distinguishes a twitch from a wink: outwardly identical contractions become legible only when layered codes — intention, convention, social recognition — are recovered by an interpreter. Thick description does not pile on detail for its own sake; it reconstructs the conditions that make an action legible as *this* action rather than another. Thick prompting borrows that distinction. A thin prompter names a subject and trusts the defaults. But defaults are what Meyer (2025) calls "platform realism" and Steyerl (2023) calls the "mean image": the statistical memory of a training corpus optimized for legibility, plausibility, and consumer expectation. Simonen et al. (2025) demonstrate empirically that default images recur across unrelated prompts when the prompt fails to grip the system. Thick prompting prunes those defaults before they harden into the result.

The practice is iterative, not declarative. Oppenlaender (2022–2024) documents prompt engineering as a community-shaped craft of modifiers, experimentation, and online ecosystems. PromptMagician (Lin et al. 2024) and the PRIP refinement model formalize the same structure: users inspect returned images and treat them as diagnostic material for the next prompt. The image is not the product. The image is feedback — a diagnostic return from behind a gate the prompter cannot see through (Oppenlaender 2024b).

Parry and Lord clarify why this is a generative practice rather than a mechanical one. Lord's singer of Homeric epic does not move like a machine through prefabricated fragments; the singer composes in performance through a grammar internalized as habit. Formulaic style enables constrained variation, not repetition. The analogy to generative AI holds at exactly one level: both rely on patterned inheritance to produce variable outputs that remain recognizably of one world. The prompter enters an archive by giving it a symptom, disturbing what it already carries. Thick prompting imports the cost of description into the generative apparatus.
