Neurosymbolic Resonance

The key concept is the preservation of structure across two generative regimes:

1. Neural / Probabilistic: LLMs, Gemini, image models, latent interpolation, platform realism.

2. Symbolic / Deterministic: BEFLIX code, ABC-Flix execution, frame-by-frame dot-matrix rendering, inspectable coordinates.

The same Homeric shield-world survives across both regimes as: zones, operations, motion, sequence, world-structure.
